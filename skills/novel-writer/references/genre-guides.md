## Science Fiction

### Core Elements
- Scientific hypothesis or technological setting
- Imagination of the future
- Technology's impact on humanity

### Subgenres
- **Hard Sci-Fi**: Emphasizes scientific accuracy
- **Soft Sci-Fi**: Focuses on society and psychology
- **Cyberpunk**: High tech, low life
- **Space Opera**: Grand interstellar adventure
- **Time Travel**: Timeline-related stories
- **Dystopia**: Oppressive future society

### Key Techniques
- Sci-fi setting must have internal logic
- Technology serves the story
- Explore essential questions about humanity

## Fantasy

### Core Elements
- Magic or supernatural powers
- Unique worldview
- Epic adventure

### Subgenres
- **High Fantasy**: Completely fictional world
- **Low Fantasy**: Fantasy elements in reality
- **Urban Fantasy**: Modern city setting
- **Dark Fantasy**: Horror meets fantasy
- **Xianxia/Xuanhuan**: Eastern cultivation system

### Key Techniques
- Magic system needs rules and costs
- Worldview must be self-consistent
- Hero's growth is central

## Mystery/Thriller

### Core Elements
- Mystery or crime
- Investigation process
- Unexpected truth

### Subgenres
- **Cozy Mystery**: Light tone, puzzle-focused
- **Hardboiled**: Gritty, private detective
- **Police Procedural**: Law enforcement perspective
- **Psychological Thriller**: Psychological suspense

### Key Techniques
- Clues must be fairly presented
- Truth must be reasonable yet surprising
- Pacing control is key

---

## Romance

### Core Elements
- Development of romantic relationship
- Realistic life details
- Relationship conflicts and resolution

### Subgenres
- **Contemporary Romance**: Modern setting
- **Historical Romance**: Historical period
- **Sweet Romance**: Light and fluffy
- **Angst Romance**: High emotional turmoil

### Key Techniques
- Characters need chemistry
- Conflicts must be believable
- Emotional development needs layers

---

## Historical/Wuxia

### Core Elements
- Historical background or wuxia world
- Martial arts or combat system
- Jianghu conflicts or historical events

### Wuxia Key Elements
- Internal and external martial arts system
- Sects and jianghu
- Chivalry spirit

### Historical Key Elements
- Period accuracy
- Historical figures and events
- Balance of fact and fiction

---

## Horror/Thriller

### Core Elements
- Source of threat (supernatural or human)
- Atmosphere of fear
- Survival or escape

### Subgenres
- **Supernatural Horror**: Ghosts and paranormal
- **Psychological Horror**: Mental terror
- **Survival Horror**: Struggle to survive
- **Thriller**: Tense excitement

### Key Techniques
- Atmosphere building more important than jump scares
- The unknown is most frightening
- Pacing and suspense control
